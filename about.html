ColdHearts Non Profit What We Do

We are a non profit organization working to make Cincinnati, Ohio a more enviromentally-friendly place to live. We will acheive this with increased awareness on the benifits of recycling by processing recycled materials into everyday, usable products. In additon, we are working with the local community to help those in need and clean up the city one piece of plastic at a time.

President and Chairman Cody Kisner

Since I was 14 years old and took my first cross-country trip out to Yosemite, I have fallen in love with all things nature. I co-founded this organization to solve some of the issues plaguing our enviroment today.

Vice President and Head of Marketing Sean Fallis

Snowboarding has always been one of my passions. The threat of global warming not only affects snowboarders, but every human on this planet. I co-founded this organization to help reduce Cincinnati's carbon footprint.

Vice President and Treasurer Isaiah Weeks

I have loved hiking for years and seeing people litter and polute our community has driven me to do something about it. I co-founded this organization to help clean up our forests and parks so everyone may continue to enjoy them.
